TL;DR: 论文比较了Kubo模型和量子场论对石墨烯电导率的理论描述，指出量子场论在空间非局域情况下存在不一致性。作者认为修改后的表达式违反规范不变性，而量子场论的结果是物理合理的。

Motivation: 比较Kubo模型和量子场论对石墨烯电导率的描述，解决量子场论在空间非局域情况下的不一致性问题。

Method: 通过比较两种理论方法，分析修改后的电导率表达式是否满足规范不变性。

Result: 量子场论的结果是物理合理的，而修改后的表达式违反规范不变性，导致非物理后果。

Conclusion: 量子场论对石墨烯电导率的描述是合理的，而修改后的表达式不可接受。

Abstract: Recently, Rodriguez-Lopez, Wang, and Antezza [arXiv:2403.02279v3; Phys. Rev.
B v.111, 115428 (2025)] compared the theoretical descriptions of electric
conductivity of graphene given by the Kubo model and quantum field theory in
terms of the polarizationtensor. According to these authors, in the spatially
nonlocal case, the quantum field theoretical description contains ``hard
inconsistencies". To bring the predictions of quantum field theory in agreement
with those following from the Kubo model, the modified expression was used
which relates the conductivity and the polarization tensor. Here, it is shown
that this modification violates the the requirement of gauge invariance and,
thus, is unacceptable. By comparing both theoretical approaches, we demonstrate
that all the results obtained within quantum field theory are physically well
justified whereas an application of the modified expression for the
conductivity of graphene leads to the consequences of nonphysical character.
